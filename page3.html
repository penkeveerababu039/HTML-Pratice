Social Media

Instagram is a popular social media platform for sharing photos, videos, and stories. Launched in 2010, it allows users to connect, follow others, and explore content through hashtags and reels. Owned by Meta, Instagram promotes creativity, social interaction, and brand marketing across personal, professional, and influencer communities worldwide. Twitter is a social media platform for sharing short messages called “tweets.” Launched in 2006, it enables users to post, reply, and follow topics or people worldwide. Known for real-time updates and trending discussions, Twitter supports communication, news sharing, and public conversations across politics, entertainment, and global events.
Facebook is a social networking platform launched in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg. It allows users to create profiles, share posts, photos, and videos, and connect with friends, family, and communities. Owned by Meta, Facebook supports messaging, groups, pages, and advertising, fostering global communication and digital social interaction. YouTube is a video-sharing platform launched in 2005, now owned by Google. It allows users to upload, watch, share, and comment on videos across various genres. From music and tutorials to vlogs and live streams, YouTube connects creators and audiences worldwide, shaping entertainment, education, and digital culture.
